Your story begins on a road to the mountains!!

You delight in the undulating smoothness of the gray road, the vibrant yellow of the tree leaves, the rich brown of the nurturing soil.

You drive by flat fields. Their open simplicity complements the sturdy mountains that climb up behind. Though there's a fence, you can't be kept in.

Sometimes there's so much beauty you have to stop and take it all in.

As you rise up from the fields, the mountains feel closer and you can smell the cypress and spruce.

You kneel by the fire, sparking kindling. It's time to warm, rest, and relax. You're wild, but not too wild.
